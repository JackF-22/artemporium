About Us


Robert Jerome and his wife bought the business in 1983, a month after their son Bryan was born. Now Bob and Bryan (pictured on the left) run the business together and do all custom framing on the premises.


We specialize in custom built picture framing. We do not sell art supplies. Please call ahead as we prefer to receive frame objects by appointment.

Special Notice to Our Customers
